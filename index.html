Italian student of the Start2Impact University. My ever-present curiosity and desire to discover the world led me to be a language student in my teenage years, managing to certify my skills with some certificates and cultural exchanges.

After school I started working as sales assistant, first in retail, then in large-scale distribution. I believe that a person should learn new things everyday, instead my last job position couldn't provide the opportunities I was looking for: I realized that I don't want only to be a part of this world, I want to shape it starting from the diversity it contains and from the desire to make it a better place.

I am an adult passionate about Philosophy, Psychology, World Cultures, with an eye always turned to the new and the different, but I am also an eternal child, an explorer of this and other worlds thanks to the power of Videogames, Cinema, and Computer Science.

I am always open to challenges,I am open-minded and learn new things quickly. I can't be certain if I want to become a Web Developer or a Web Designer: not enough professional experience to be certain about that.

What I know for sure is that I want to dedicate all the skills I am learning, along with my life experiences, to create unique products and keep alive the sense of wonder in the world.
